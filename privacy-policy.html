Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we protect your data.

Introduction

At Healr ("we", "our", or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application designed for physiotherapists.

Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. The only information we collect is:

  • Mobile Phone Number: Required for account login and authentication purposes only.

We do not collect any other personal information such as names, email addresses, location data, or browsing history.

How We Store Your Information

Your security is our priority. Here's how we protect your data:

  • Encryption: Your mobile number is encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols before being stored.
  • Secure Storage: All data is stored on secure servers with strict access controls.
  • No Plain Text: We never store your mobile number in plain text format.

Data Access & Privacy

We have implemented strict data access controls to ensure privacy:

  • Physiotherapist Data Isolation: Each physiotherapist can only view and access their own patient details. You cannot see data belonging to other physiotherapists.
  • Patient Privacy: Patient information entered by a physiotherapist is only accessible to that specific physiotherapist.
  • No Cross-Access: There is no way for one user to access another user's patient records or data.

Information Sharing

We want to be absolutely clear about our data sharing practices:

  • We do NOT sell your information to anyone.
  • We do NOT share your information with third parties.
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising purposes.
  • We do NOT provide your data to data brokers or marketing companies.

Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as you maintain an active account with Healr. If you choose to delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request information about what data we have stored.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: You can request an export of your data.

Security Measures

We employ various security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted storage for all sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
